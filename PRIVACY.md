# Hidden Gems — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 14, 2026_

Hidden Gems ("the app") is a social iOS app for discovering and sharing
restaurant recommendations. This policy describes what information the
app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

## Information we collect

When you create an account and use Hidden Gems, we collect:

- **Account information** — the email address and password you use to
  sign up, plus the display name and username you choose during profile
  setup.
- **Content you create** — the restaurant posts, photos, comments,
  likes, follows, and saved restaurants you submit through the app.
- **Device information needed to run the app** — your device's coarse
  location (only when you explicitly grant permission for
  location-aware features).

We do **not** collect advertising identifiers, contacts, health data,
financial information, or tracking data across other apps and websites.

## How we use your information

We use the information above solely to operate the app: authenticating
you, showing your posts to other users, powering the social feed, and
personalizing your experience based on who you follow. We do not sell
your information to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

## Service providers

Hidden Gems uses the following services to operate the app:

- **Supabase** — stores your account, profile, posts, comments, likes,
  and follows. Supabase is a data processor that hosts this data on our
  behalf. See [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **Unsplash** — some restaurant photos shown in the app are loaded
  directly from Unsplash's CDN. See
  [unsplash.com/privacy](https://unsplash.com/privacy).

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

## Data retention

Your account and the content you create are retained until you request
deletion. If you want to delete your account, email the address below
and we will delete your account and associated content within 30 days.

## Your choices

You can:

- Edit your display name and username from your profile.
- Sign out at any time from your profile.
- Request account deletion by emailing the address below.

## Children

Hidden Gems is not directed at children under 13, and we do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
